Mayday!

Automatic emergency response in your pocket

Mayday! is a state-of-the-art automatic emergency response system for your smart phone.

Mayday! looks after you - so you can enjoy the journey ahead.

Key Features

Automatic Emergency Response

Mayday! contacts Emergency Response in case of an accident reducing time of arrival by up to 5 minutes and increasing the chance of survival by up to 20%.

Sensor Fusion

Mayday! fuses information provided by the accelerometer, compass, GPS, and gyroscope of your smart-phone to reliably detect accidents and minimise false alarms.

Battery Life

Mayday!'s efficient implementation doesn't drain the battery unnecessarily - meaning you can ride for longer and arrive with juice in your phone.

Background Operation

Mayday! runs in the background. You can use you maps to navigate your way or answer phone calls - all while Mayday! is looking after you.
