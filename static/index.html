Automatic Emergency Response

ride.withflare directly hails Emergency Services and your loved ones if an accident occurs. The first 5 minutes are crucial. ride.withflare makes them count.

Sensor Fusion 2.0

ride.withflare fuses your phone's sensors together with our situational awareness technology to reliably detect accidents and prevent false alarms.

Battery Life

ride.withflare's optimised design doesn't drain the battery unnecessarily - meaning you can ride for longer and arrive with juice in your phone.

Background Operation

ride.withflare runs in the background. You can use maps to navigate your way or answer phone calls - all while ride.withflare is looking after you.

How It Works

1

Monitor Vehicle Dynamics

ride.withflare observes your vehicle dynamics - velocity, acceleration, angular velocity, and lean angle.

2

Automatically Classify Accidents

Our classifier technology fuses sensor, vehicular and situational data to detect accidents the instant they happen.

3

Notify Emergency Contacts

A distress signal is raised to emergency services with your location, medical status and accident data.

4

Get Medical Attention - Stat!

Emergency Response Services arrive at the scene sooner, together with the information they need.
