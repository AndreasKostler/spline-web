Key Features

Automatic Emergency Response

withflare contacts Emergency Response in case of an accident reducing time of arrival by up to 5 minutes and increasing the chance of survival by up to 20%.

Sensor Fusion

withflare fuses information provided by the accelerometer, compass, GPS, and gyroscope of your smart-phone to reliably detect accidents and minimise false alarms.

Battery Life

withflare's efficient implementation doesn't drain the battery unnecessarily - meaning you can ride for longer and arrive with juice in your phone.

Background Operation

withflare runs in the background. You can use you maps to navigate your way or answer phone calls - all while withflare is looking after you.

How It Works

1

Monitor Vehicle Dynamics

While You are enjoying the ride, withflare monitors your vehicle dynamics - velocity, acceleration, angular velocity, and lean angle.

2

Automatically Classify Accidents

Withflare's built-in classifier will detect accidents the instant they happen.

3

Notify Emergency Contacts

A distress call is sent out to emergency response services within seconds containing your last know location and medical data such as blood type and known allergies.

4

Get the Medical Attention You Need - Fast

Emergency Response Services arrive at the scene faster and with all the information they need.
